# ⚡ CareerFluxy – Smarter Way to Crack Interviews

> 💡 Not just preparation… **CareerFluxy is your personal interview coach.**

---

## 🚀 About the Project

**CareerFluxy** is a modern AI-powered platform designed to help students and job seekers prepare, practice, and perform better in interviews.

It combines **AI simulation, resume tools, and smart learning** — all in one place.

---

## 🎯 Why CareerFluxy?

❌ Traditional preparation is confusing
❌ No real interview practice
❌ Resume mistakes go unnoticed

✅ CareerFluxy solves all of this with **real-time tools + AI guidance**

---

## ✨ Core Features

🔥 **AI Mock Interview**

> Experience real interview scenarios with intelligent feedback

📄 **Resume Builder**

> Create clean, professional resumes in minutes

🔍 **Resume Analyzer**

> Get smart suggestions to improve your resume instantly

📚 **Smart Question Bank**

> Practice from beginner to advanced level questions

🧑‍💼 **Admin Panel**

> Manage users, content & system easily

🎥 **Video Resume (Coming Soon)**

> Stand out with modern hiring trends

---

## 🧠 How It Works

1. 👤 User signs up / logs in
2. 🎯 Selects domain (IT, Banking, etc.)
3. 🤖 Takes AI mock interview
4. 📊 Gets performance analysis
5. 📄 Builds & improves resume
